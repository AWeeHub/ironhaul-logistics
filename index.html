03 — FAQ

Common Questions

How quickly can IronHaul pick up my freight?
Most loads can be picked up within 24–48 hours of booking confirmation. For time-critical shipments, ask about our expedited service — we maintain a dedicated pool of available carriers on standby across all 48 contiguous states at all times.
Can I track my shipment in real time?
Yes. Every IronHaul shipment includes GPS tracking with live ETAs via your customer portal. You'll also receive automated SMS and email updates at every key milestone — pickup, departure, estimated arrival, and proof of delivery.
What commodities do you NOT haul?
IronHaul does not haul hazardous materials requiring Class 1–9 HazMat placards on standard agreements, live animals, or household goods. For controlled substances or specialty commodities, contact our compliance team before booking.
What are your payment and invoicing terms?
Standard terms are net-30 from date of delivery for approved credit accounts. We accept ACH, wire transfer, and major credit cards. New shippers may remit on first delivery. Quick-pay options with a small discount are available through the portal.
